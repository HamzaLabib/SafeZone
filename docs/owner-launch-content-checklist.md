# Owner Launch Content Checklist

Safe Zone Security Academy can continue preview testing with request-based flows, but these owner-supplied items are still needed before public launch, payment activation, or full e-commerce.

## Business Identity

- [ ] Official legal business name.
- [ ] NEQ, if applicable.
- [ ] Approved tagline or slogan.
- [ ] Confirmed website domain.
- [ ] Final service area wording.
- [ ] Main business email confirmation.
- [ ] Public phone number.
- [ ] Physical address or confirmed no-public-address wording.
- [ ] Business/admissions hours.

## Courses

- [ ] Final list of launch courses.
- [ ] Course prices.
- [ ] Course schedules/cohorts.
- [ ] Course duration confirmation.
- [ ] Course format: in-person, online, or hybrid.
- [ ] Course location or online delivery details.
- [ ] Prerequisites and required documents.
- [ ] Certificate/credential wording.
- [ ] Instructor names, bios, photos, and credentials.
- [ ] BSP/licensing wording reviewed and approved.

## Policies And Legal

- [ ] Privacy policy approved for Quebec/Canada requirements.
- [ ] Terms of use approved.
- [ ] Refund policy approved.
- [ ] Cancellation/rescheduling policy approved.
- [ ] French-language decision and final French content, if required.
- [ ] Consent wording approved for contact, registration interest, and item request forms.
- [ ] Tax handling confirmed with accountant for GST/QST.
- [ ] Distance-selling disclosures confirmed before any online checkout.

## Shop / Market

- [ ] Final product list.
- [ ] Product descriptions.
- [ ] Product prices.
- [ ] Product photos.
- [ ] Availability/inventory expectations.
- [ ] Pickup, shipping, or manual fulfillment method.
- [ ] Refund/return handling for products.
- [ ] Which items are taxable.

## Payments

- [ ] Client-owned Stripe account.
- [ ] Payment recipient bank account.
- [ ] Approved course/product prices.
- [ ] Approved refund/cancellation terms.
- [ ] Approval to start test-mode Stripe Checkout integration.
- [ ] Approval to keep shop request-only until full e-commerce is planned.

## Assets And Marketing

- [ ] Image/source licensing confirmed for all public images.
- [ ] Real product photos provided or placeholders approved for preview only.
- [ ] Google Business Profile details: business name, category, address/service area, phone, hours, photos, website, and description.
- [ ] Testimonials or trust signals, if available and approved.
